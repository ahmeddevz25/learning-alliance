The Cambridge O Level Programme at Learning Alliance follows Ministry of Education requirements and is taught by experienced subject specialists. Our purpose-built academic block supports coursework and gives students the space to explore their strengths. Students take 5 compulsory subjects and choose either 3 optional subjects or one full subject group.

A Level

Students in Pakistan take AS/A Levels for local and international university admissions.

Learning Alliance offers 17 A Level subjects and the option to complete the qualification through a staged route: AS in one exam session, A Level the next year. (Urdu is the only exception.) We register students for the June exam series, with results released in August.
