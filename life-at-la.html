Learning Alliance and Learning Alliance International Proclamation and High Performers Award Ceremonies are annual events held at the start of each academic year to celebrate students’ achievements from the previous year across academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.

Students receive awards and special council medals, with parents invited to attend alongside an art exhibition showcasing student work. The ceremony also honors the dedication of the faculty and concludes with the House Trophy announcement, school song, and confetti.
