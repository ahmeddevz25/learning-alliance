Introduction

Learning Alliance delivers high-quality education, world-class resources, and an environment that empowers students to become independent, curious, and self-driven learners.

Our mission is to inspire exploration, informed decision-making, and self-awareness while shaping students into responsible, respectful, and active members of their communities.

Our faculty is carefully chosen for their expertise and dedication to developing well-rounded learners, guiding each student's academic and personal growth. Learning Alliance is a co-educational institution offering IGCSE, O Levels, and A Levels through the Cambridge Assessment International Education Board.

Our Campuses

With campuses in Aziz Avenue Lahore, DHA Lahore, and Amir Town Faisalabad, Learning Alliance offers strong academics and diverse extracurriculars. The school year runs from August to June, with a two-month summer break and holidays in December and March.
