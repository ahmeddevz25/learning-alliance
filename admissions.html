Required Documents

  • Applicant's recent Passport size Photograph
  • Applicant's Hospital Birth Certificate
  • Applicant's B-Form/NICOP/Smart Card or Passport (for foreign citizens)
  • Family Registration Certificate
  • Applicant's previous School Report (if applicable)
  • Applicant's School Leaving Certificate (if applicable)
  • CNIC or NICOP for both Parents
  • Certificates pertaining to the applicant's Cocurricular Achievements (if applicable)

CAIE CLASSES I - VIII ADMISSIONS

Registration forms are available against vacant seats only. Forms may be purchased at the Admissions Office at all three campuses. Registrations will be only be entertained if the form has been provided by the School Office.

Required Documents

  • Registration form duly completed
  • Copy of the birth certificate
  • Copy of B-Form
  • Two passport size photographs with the child's name written on the back of both photographs
  • Copy of Last academic report card (if applicable)
  • Copy of both parents CNIC

Incorrect information provided may render the registration void. Registration does not guarantee admission.

O LEVEL ADMISSIONS

Students may register against vacant seats only, at the Learning Alliance DHA Admissions Head Office. Registrations will be only be entertained if the form has been provided by the Admissions Office.
Required Documents Registration Fee Conditions

Required Documents

  • Complete registration form
  • Copy of Hospital birth certificate
  • Copy of B-Form
  • Two passport size photographs (child's name written on the back of both photos)
  • Copy of last academic report card (if applicable)
  • Copy of both parents CNIC

Any false information provided by the parent or the student may render the registration void. Registration does not guarantee admission.

A LEVEL ADMISSIONS

A Level Registrations for each new Academic Year begin in June. Parents may visit the Admissions Office during school hours to learn more and begin the process.

Required Documents

  • Registration form duly completed
  • Copy of the birth certificate
  • Copy of B-Form
  • Two passport size photographs with the child's name written on the back of both photographs
  • Copy of Last academic report card (if applicable)
  • Copy of both parents CNIC

Incorrect information provided may render the registration void. Registration does not guarantee admission.
